Our History

The journey of Udiyaman Jubasangha

Udiyaman Jubasangha was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate young individuals who wanted to make a difference in their community. What started as a small gathering of like-minded youth has grown into a vibrant organization with over 64 members.

Over the years, we have organized numerous community service projects, educational programs, and social events that have positively impacted thousands of people in our community.

Our commitment to youth empowerment and community development has remained unwavering throughout our journey, and we continue to expand our reach and impact.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us forward

Mission

To empower youth through community engagement, leadership development, and social service, creating a positive impact on society and fostering personal growth.

Vision

A society where young people are active contributors to community development, equipped with the skills, knowledge, and values to lead positive change.

Values

Integrity, Compassion, Inclusivity, Innovation, and Excellence guide all our activities and interactions.

Our Goals

What we strive to achieve

Educational Support

Provide educational resources and mentoring to underprivileged students to help them succeed academically.

Community Development

Implement projects that address community needs and improve the quality of life for local residents.

Leadership Development

Cultivate leadership skills among youth through workshops, training programs, and practical experiences.

Social Service

Organize regular volunteer activities that address social issues and promote a culture of giving back.
